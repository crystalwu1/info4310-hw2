What students go to college far from home?

Lydia Kim (lmk225), Crystal Wu (cw683)

In 1980, the National Center for Education Statistics conducted a longitudinal survey to observe the development of young people. We used a cross-section of the data from the original survey to investigate patterns in how far students moved to attend 4-year colleges and universities.
